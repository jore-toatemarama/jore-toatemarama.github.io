How do I see myself?

I am an honest and kind person. I love being creative and see my enjoyment for coding as an extension of that. I am curious and I love learning new things and understanding the world and what’s happening around me. However, I have a tendency to be overly critical, specifically of myself. This has the potential to make whatever path I choose to take more difficult than it needs to be so I try to be aware of when this is becoming a problem and be more kind and understanding of my own growth. I am still learning how to manage this. I am currently undiagnosed (due to the difficulty and cost) but believe I may have ADHD, this has the tendency to be helpful in certain situations but also a hindrance in others, its tricky to know which it will be unless the situation is familiar, which can make me afraid of trying new things
